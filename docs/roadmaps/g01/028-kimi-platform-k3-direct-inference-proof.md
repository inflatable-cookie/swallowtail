## Bounded First Proof

One Kimi Platform API-key instance at the `api.moonshot.ai` audience exposes a
bounded authenticated model catalogue and one resource-free text structured
run. The run pins `kimi-k3`, requires a positive output-token bound and exact
`low`, `high`, or `max` reasoning selection, streams reasoning separately from
final output, observes terminal usage, validates the returned model, and makes
one attempt.

The driver claims no tools, structured output, multimodal input, files, batch,
balance, official tools, search, Partial Mode, provider history, retry,
reconnection, subscription, or fallback. Cancellation and deadline stop local
connection work; they do not claim native provider cancellation.
